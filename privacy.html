Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to LifeMind. LifeMind is a personal health and fitness iOS application developed by Adriana Mueller ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the LifeMind app.

We take your privacy seriously. LifeMind is designed with a privacy-first architecture: the vast majority of your personal and health data never leaves your device. We do not operate servers that store your health data, and we do not sell your personal information to any third party.

By using LifeMind, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

LifeMind collects two broad categories of information: (a) data you enter and data obtained from Apple HealthKit that is stored entirely on your device, and (b) limited data transmitted to third-party services when you use specific optional features.

2a. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored solely on your iPhone. This data is never transmitted to LifeMind servers because no such servers exist:

  • Personal Profile: First name, date of birth, and city/location provided during onboarding.
  • Health Metrics: Steps, active calories burned, resting heart rate, and body weight — synced from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission.
  • Nutrition Data: Food logs including meal entries, calorie counts, macronutrients, and daily water intake.
  • Sleep Data: Bedtime, wake time, and sleep quality scores you log or that are pulled from HealthKit.
  • Workout Logs: Exercise sessions including exercise name, sets, reps, weight used, RPE, and session type.
  • Wellness Data: Supplement intake records, fasting session times, body soreness map data, and skin quality scores.
  • Progress Photos: Before/after photos you take within the app. Stored only on your device and never uploaded.
  • Calendar Events: LifeMind events you create, optionally exported to Apple Calendar on your request.
  • Country Preference: Your selected country for public holiday display.

2b. Data Transmitted to Third-Party Services

Certain optional features require sending limited data to external services. This only occurs when you actively use those features:

  • Weather (optional): Your device's GPS coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to fetch local weather. LifeMind does not store or log your location data.
  • Food Search: Search queries are sent to Open Food Facts and/or USDA FoodData Central to retrieve nutritional information.
  • Public Holidays: Your selected country code is sent to nager.at to retrieve public holiday dates.
  • AI Features (optional, own API key required): If you enable AI features and provide your own API key for Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok, your messages are sent directly to the respective provider. LifeMind does not proxy, store, or view these messages. Your API key is stored locally on your device in the iOS Keychain.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected by LifeMind is used exclusively to provide your personal health tracking experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app features you use. We do not share your data with data brokers or marketing platforms.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple HealthKit — Reads health metrics with your explicit iOS permission. apple.com/privacy
  • Open-Meteo — Current weather data. GDPR-compliant, non-commercial. open-meteo.com
  • Open Food Facts — Food and nutrition database. openfoodfacts.org/privacy
  • USDA FoodData Central — Nutritional data. No personal data transmitted beyond your search query.
  • Nager.at — Public holiday data by country. Only your country code is transmitted.
  • Firebase (Google) — Anonymous authentication to uniquely identify your device session without linking to your identity. firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Sentry — Crash and performance monitoring. No personally identifiable information is transmitted (sendDefaultPii = false). sentry.io/privacy
  • AI APIs (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI) — If you use AI features with your own API key, your messages go directly to the chosen provider. LifeMind acts only as a local interface.

5. Data Storage & Security

All personal and health data is stored locally on your iOS device. LifeMind does not operate any backend servers, databases, or cloud storage that receives or retains your data.

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security: iOS hardware encryption, Face ID / Touch ID, and the iOS app sandboxing model. API keys you enter for AI services are stored in the iOS Keychain and are never transmitted to LifeMind.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Because all your LifeMind data resides on your device, you have complete control at all times.

  • You can delete individual logs, entries, or records from within the app at any time.
  • Deleting the LifeMind app permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • HealthKit data synced via Apple Health remains in the Health app and can be managed there.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account within the app.

7. Your Rights under GDPR

If you are in the EEA, Switzerland, or the UK, you have rights under the GDPR. As LifeMind stores data locally on your device, most rights are exercised directly within the app. To exercise any rights or raise privacy concerns, contact us at support@lifemind.app. We will respond within 30 days.

The relevant supervisory authority is the Commission de Contrôle des Informations Nominatives (CCIN) — ccin.mc.

8. Children's Privacy

LifeMind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The THEA AI nutrition coaching feature is intended for users aged 18 and over.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app's features or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the Effective Date and notify you through the app or App Store update notes.
